Our Story

I am a dedicated jewellery designer specialising in bespoke, hand-knotted cord bracelets that reflect individuality, craftsmanship, and minimalist elegance. With a strong focus on technical precision and material durability, each piece is thoughtfully designed and carefully handcrafted using premium-grade cords to meet the highest standards of wearable art.


My work is driven by a passion for transforming simple, high-quality materials into refined, everyday luxury. I collaborate closely with clients to select the perfect cord textures and custom metallic accents, ensuring every bracelet captures their personal vision while maintaining a sophisticated and enduring aesthetic.


From the selection of the cord to the final securing of the knot, every detail is considered to deliver jewellery that is both resilient and meaningful. My goal is to provide a professional, seamless experience while creating custom cord pieces that are designed to be treasured and worn for years to come.

Specialist Services

  • Premium Grade Cord Materials
  • Custom Colour Combinations
  • Engraved Metallic Accents
